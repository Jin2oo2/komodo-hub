Welcome to Komodo Hub

Welcome to Komodo Hub, your gateway to the world of conservation and wildlife protection in Indonesia. We are the Komodo Foundation, a passionate team of conservationists, educators, and tech enthusiasts dedicated to making a difference for our environment.

At Komodo Hub, we believe in the power of technology to bring people, schools, and communities together in the mission to protect and preserve Indonesia's endangered species. Our journey began with a vision to create a digital platform that empowers individuals, schools, and communities to actively participate in conservation programs, share their knowledge, and make a positive impact on our precious wildlife.

With the support of the Yayasan Komodo (Komodo Foundation) and the unwavering commitment of our team, we've embarked on a groundbreaking initiative to build a comprehensive platform that offers a wealth of learning materials, interactive programs, and a safe space for students, teachers, and conservation enthusiasts to connect, learn, and take action.

Join us in this exciting endeavor as we work together to protect the biodiversity of Indonesia and promote environmental education. Every contribution, no matter how big or small, matters.

Together, we can make a lasting impact and ensure a brighter future for our endangered species.

Let's embark on this incredible journey of discovery, learning, and conservation. Welcome to Komodo Hub!
